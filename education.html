High School

Attended Walkerville Collegiate Institute from September 2008 until June 2013 and received my Ontario Secondary School Diploma with Specialist High Skills Major in Music in 2012. To obtain a Specialist High Skills Major on an OSSD a student must obtain credits for various courses in a specific discipline. For me this was music. I specialized in guitar, trumpet, saxophone and piano during my time at WCI. I also took classes on music theory, composition, orchestration and took a co-op where I taught music at a gradeschool to all ages and students who are differently abled. I returned to attend WCI after I had graduated to obtain extra courses in math and science so I could attend the University of Windsor.

University

Attended the University of Windsor for two years from September 2013 until June 2015 and received credits for various courses such as: physics, chemistry, math, philosophy and astronomy. I ended up deciding on a different career path for myself and took a year off to work a bit before continuing with my education.

College
